Pizza

Description

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

Ingredients

  • Warm water
  • Active dry yeast
  • White sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Minced garlic
  • Fresh basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Black olives
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Make dough
  3. Mix in flour
  4. Knead dough
  5. Cover dough with damp cloth
  6. Punch dough and set aside
  7. Make garlic oil
  8. Preheat grill
  9. Make pizza
  10. Put toppings on pizza
