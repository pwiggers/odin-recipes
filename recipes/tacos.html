Tacos

Description

These Easy Vegetarian Tacos are filled with a rainbow of vegetables, black beans, and lots of fresh and delicious toppings! They make a great quick, easy, and healthy meal! You can have them on the dinner table in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

Basic Ingredients

  • Olive oil or avocado oil
  • Veggies - red onion, zucchini, mushrooms, red bell pepper, and garlic! A rainbow of veggies! And of course, feel free to use your favorite veggies or whatever you have on hand! All veggies are good in these tacos!
  • Spices & Herbs - ground cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, and dried oregano
  • Black beans - I use canned black beans to keep things super simple!
  • Lime juice - use fresh limes!
  • Cilantro - chop up fresh cilantro for lots of flavor!

Directions

Basic Directions

  1. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium high heat. Add all of the veggies and cook until the vegetables are softened, stirring occasionally. Add in the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Stir in the cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, oregano, and a little bit of water. The water will help everything come together.
  3. Carefully stir in the black beans, making sure you don't smash them. Add the lime juice and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Now, it's time to assemble the tacos! Place the filling in the center of warm corn tortillas and top with desired toppings!
